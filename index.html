Spndle
spndle.com ↗ App Store ↗

Expense tracking for folks with side-hustles who need Schedule C records but not QuickBooks. Point the camera at a receipt and the AI reads the payee, amount, and description automatically, and gives its best guess for Schedule C category. GPS mileage tracking, PDF/CSV export, multiple businesses, household mode, recurring expenses. All data lives on YOUR device. $9.99/year.

StillOnIt stillonit.com ↗

Lightweight desktop time tracker for Mac and Windows. Lives in the menu bar. A small popup appears at your chosen interval. Still on the same task? Ignore it. Switching? Tap the new one. The weekly report shows you what you need for Harvest, Toggl, Clockify, or whatever you're using. Local SQLite, no account, free.

Claude Code Flutter / Dart SQLite macOS Windows System tray
iCountdownClock
iCountdownClock.com ↗ App Store ↗

Apple Watch + iPhone countdown app. I wrote this to remind me when Midterms are rolling around! Track upcoming events as scrollable tiles on your watch; pin one as a watch face complication. Show on your phone as a widget. What sets it apart: it monitors your HRV in the background, and when stress is detected it quietly reminds you of the next thing you're looking forward to, rather than telling you that you're stressed.

Claude Code Swift / SwiftUI watchOS WidgetKit HealthKit (HRV) StoreKit
